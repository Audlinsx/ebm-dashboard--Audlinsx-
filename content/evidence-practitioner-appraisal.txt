*Systematic evaluation of expert knowledge credibility and implementation guidance quality*

rating** with strong applicability to current organizational context and robust implementation framework validation.

### 6.3 Evidence Limitations and Considerations

**Acknowledged Limitations:**
- **Geographic Bias:** 82% of expert experience in North American healthcare systems
- **Setting Bias:** 73% of expert experience in academic medical centers and large health systems
- **Success Bias:** Experts may overemphasize positive outcomes from their own successful projects
- **Temporal Bias:** Limited long-term (>3 year) outcome data for sustainability assessment

**Risk Mitigation for Limitations:**
- **Diverse Expert Base:** Included international experts and community hospital experience
- **Failure Case Analysis:** Systematically included failed implementation examples
- **Anonymous Input:** Survey data provides unattributed insights reducing social desirability bias
- **Cross-Validation:** Expert recommendations validated against multiple evidence sources

# ASSESS: Comprehensive Assessment & Monitoring Framework
# Systematic evaluation of shared governance implementation effectiveness

## Logic Model Framework (X → M → Y)

### **X (Independent Variable): Shared Governance Implementation**

| **Input Components** | **Activities** | **Outputs** |
|---------------------|---------------|-------------|
| **Financial:** $600K budget | **Council Setup:** Unit + organizational councils | **Functional Councils:** 5 councils, 28 trained members |
| **Leadership:** Executive sponsorship + resources | **Training:** Leadership + staff development (200+ hrs) | **Trained Staff:** 90%+ completion rate |
| **Change Mgmt:** Professional consulting + coaching | **Communication:** Multi-channel feedback systems | **Enhanced Comm:** Bi-weekly updates + feedback loops |
| **Protected Time:** 6 hrs monthly per member | **Policy Participation:** Decision authority delegation | **Active Participation:** 75%+ attendance rates |

### **M (Mediator Variables): Intermediate Outcomes Chain**

| **Mediator Variable** | **Measurement Method** | **Timeline** | **Target Change** |
|----------------------|----------------------|-------------|------------------|
| **M1: Staff Engagement** | Monthly engagement surveys (5-point scale) | Months 1-18 | 2.9 → 3.5 (21% improvement) |
| **M2: Communication Effectiveness** | Communication quality assessments + feedback | Months 1-18 | 2.7 → 3.8 (41% improvement) |
| **M3: Professional Autonomy** | Professional autonomy scale + participation metrics | Months 1-18 | 3.1 → 3.7 (19% improvement) |
| **M4: Management Effectiveness** | Leadership competency assessments + 360 feedback | Months 1-18 | Enhanced shared governance decision-making skills |

### **Y (Dependent Variables): Final Outcomes**

| **Primary Outcome** | **Measurement Approach** | **Baseline** | **Target** | **Timeline** |
|--------------------|-------------------------|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| **Y1: Turnover Reduction** | HR turnover data + exit interviews | 22.1% annual | 16% annual (30% reduction) | 12-18 months |
| **Y2: Patient Satisfaction** | HCAHPS communication scores + internal surveys | 68.2% | 73.2% (5-point improvement) | 9-15 months |
| **Y3: Financial Performance** | Cost analysis + ROI calculation | $1.247M costs | $905K annual benefits (302% ROI) | 12-18 months |
| **Y4: Safety Outcomes** | Communication-related safety events tracking | 35% of events | ≤25% of events (29% reduction) | 6-12 months |

### **Causal Pathway Logic**
**X (Shared Governance) → M1 (↑Staff Engagement) → M2 (↑Communication) → M3 (↑Autonomy) → M4 (↑Management) → Y (↑Retention, ↑Satisfaction, ↑ROI, ↑Safety)**

### **Implementation Fidelity Monitoring System**

**Observation Protocol:**
- **Council Meeting Observations:** Monthly structured observations using standardized checklist
- **Decision-Making Process Reviews:** Quarterly assessment of authority delegation and participation
- **Training Session Monitoring:** Real-time competency assessment and skill demonstration
- **Communication Effectiveness Audits:** Monthly review of information flow and feedback responsiveness

**Survey-Based Fidelity Assessment:**
- **Participant Experience Surveys:** Monthly pulse surveys measuring meaningful participation and authority
- **Leadership Support Surveys:** Quarterly assessment of executive commitment and resource provision
- **Communication Quality Surveys:** Bi-weekly feedback on information clarity and responsiveness
- **Training Effectiveness Surveys:** Post-training competency self-assessment and supervisor validation
